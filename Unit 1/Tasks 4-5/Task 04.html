Popular Tourist Destinations

1. Taj Mahal

The Taj Mahal is one of the Seven Wonders of the World located in Agra, India. It is famous for its beautiful white marble architecture.

2. Goa

Goa is known for its beautiful beaches, nightlife, and Portuguese heritage.

3. Kashmir

Kashmir is famous for its scenic mountains, lakes, and natural beauty.
